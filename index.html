WHAT IS A FUNGAL INFECTION

A fungus that invades the tissue can cause a disease that is confined to the skin,
spread into tissue,bones and organs or affects the whole body.

A fungi infection also called a mycosis, is a skin disease caused by a fungus.
there are millions of species of fungi.
They live in the dirt,on plants,on household surfaces, and on your skin.
Sometimes they can lead to skin problems likes rashes or bumps.

TYPES OF FUNGI INFECTIONS

(1)Althlete's foot
(2)Jock itch(3)Ringworm
(4)Yeast infections
